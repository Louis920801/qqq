Four-Seam
Introduce
The four-seam fastball means that after the ball is thrown, the ball will be seen in the direction of the hitter, and there are four almost parallel seams that rotate towards the direction of the hitter every revolution. Among them, some people think that if the ball rotates in four almost parallel seams, but there is no obvious tail force in the trajectory, we will call this ball path like this.
